~Painless Props - Garage Package~
~by Little Arms Studios, LLC~

This Painless Props Garage Packages contains low-poly models, textures, materials, and prefabs of various objects you would find in a bedroom. It also contains a showcase scene as an example of how the props might be used to decorate a scene.

List of finished objects:
-Circuit Breaker
-Garage Shelf
-Hulahoop
-Motor Oil
-Portable Vacuum
-Shopvac
-Skateboard
-Tool Drawer
-Toolbox
-Vacuum Cleaner
-Vacuum Robot
-Yoga Ball

How to Use:
To place a Painless Prop in your scene, open the Prefabs folder in your Project Pane under Painless Props/Bedroom. Click your desired object prefab and drag it into your scene.
